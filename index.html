I'm Chris, a graduate student
at Northwestern University.

I build robots that help people.

I aspire to work within and lead teams that will build robots that make our world better. My past experiences - ranging from programming robots to help people navigate their world to designing circuits for wheel-to-track transformer robots - have afforded me with a diverse background in robotics.

When I'm not building robots, I'm cooking something overly complicated, updating my collection of spreadsheets, or maintaining my map of restaurant reviews.
